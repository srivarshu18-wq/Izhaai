Ocean & Earth Dual Concept

Born from the perfect balance of nature's two most abundant sources, Izhaai combines seaweed from the ocean with pineapple fiber from the earth.

Ocean (Seaweed): Natural, nutrient-rich, and sustainably harvested for superior comfort and care.

Earth (Pineapple Fiber): An agricultural by-product transformed into premium, eco-friendly material.
